About Skill Forge

  Forge began with a realization that in college, we were taught how to pass exams, but not always how to build, explore, or think like engineers. Recognizing that showed us what we needed to create for ourselves.

  This community exists because of shared effort. Students who learned something new and chose to pass it on instead of keeping it to themselves. Every session, discussion, and resource here is shaped by that spirit.

  Our goal is simple and collective: to walk out not just as "Graduates" but as "Engineers"! We do this by sharing what we learned to our peers and building ourselves together

  We aim to bring the joy in building projects, working together, sharing knowledge, and much more.
